About me

My name is Liliana Hernandez and I am majoring in animation and motioon graphics at BMCC!

I am currently studying to be a character designer for an animation studio or to be freelance artist. A majority of my art work is traditional, however, I do work digitally if needed. I prefer to use charcoal, pencil, or acylic paint to create my work. I am capable of teaching techniques with either medium as well!

The projects showcased on this website come from of my introduction to multimedia class.
